About Me

My journey into the world of Game Art began with zero experience and a strong curiosity to create. Starting from scratch, I dedicated myself to learning the fundamentals of drawing and digital art, teaching myself through online tutorials, practice, and trial-and-error. This process allowed me to gradually develop my ability to bring characters and environments to life with vibrant colors, proportions, and expressions.

In parallel, I discovered Spine2D, a powerful tool for 2D skeletal animation, and committed to mastering it through self-study. Learning Spine2D enabled me to understand the principles of smooth and dynamic animation, helping me create lifelike character movements that enhance gameplay and storytelling. This hands-on experience deepened my appreciation of how animation breathes life into static art.

Although the path was challenging, balancing art fundamentals with technical animation skills has shaped me into a versatile Game Artist. I’m passionate about continuing to grow, eager to contribute creatively and technically to game projects that captivate players and push the boundaries of interactive art.
